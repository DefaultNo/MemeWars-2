Pass all the Bosses

Defeat all the bosses and level up to the max. Get through the game and get pieces of unique characters from the world of MEME. Play the map 5 times to knock out 5 skin fragments and get them in your arsenal for powerful victories! Upgrade all levels and earn real money.

The Olympics

Feel the real adrenaline! Fight with other players 1 on 1. Fight your way through 3 rounds and win the battle. Win 2 out of 3 rounds to get into the Olympics leaderboard and get tokens for winning. Every week additional prizes will be drawn among the leaders. For equal chances to win, the game will automatically pick an opponent and > give random weapons for battles. Every fighter has the same chance to win. Only your spirit, skill and will to win will make you a true winner.
